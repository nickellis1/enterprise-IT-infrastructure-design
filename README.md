# Enterprise IT infrastructure design
Senior Capstone Project - IT Consulting Simulation

## Overview
Our team served as IT consultants tasked with designing a complete enterprise IT infrastructure for the new, 100-employee, corporate office of Geezertown Investments, a fictitious company focused on personalized retirement planning for senior clients. 

Across 5 milestone reports, we designed the main office’s **LAN and VoIP system**, specified **enterprise-grade hardware and software** for 100 employees, and developed **backup, redundancy, and continuity plans** to support secure, scalable business operations.


## Technical Scope & Applied Concepts
-	Enterprise LAN design and VLAN segmentation
-	VoIP system integration within a secure network
-	Business and stakeholder requirements analysis
-	Hardware and software specifications for enterprise environments
-	Archival, backup, redundancy, and disaster recovery planning
-	Endpoint and data security planning (encryption, access control, network segmentation)
-	Technical documentation and vendor comparison
-	Cross-team collaboration in infrastructure design

## Milestone Report Breakdown

### Milestone 1 – Business Analysis and Risk Assessment
-	Established business operations and technical requirements
-	Documented assumptions and project scope
-	Identified key stakeholders and their priorities
-	Analyzed potential contingencies and operational risks
### Milestone 2 – Network & Telephony Design
-	Designed the core LAN infrastructure for the corporate office
-	Defined VLAN structure and network segmentation
-	Designed a VoIP-based telephone system with secure LAN integration
### Milestone 3 – Employee Equipment (Hardware & Software Specifications)
-	Specified hardware and software requirements for employees and IT staff
-	Selected enterprise-grade solutions for systems, devices, and management tools
### Milestone 4 –  Backup & Continuity Plan
-	Designed a backup and continuity plan
-	Specified off-site storage for data archives
-	Planned redundancy measures including UPS systems and generator support
## Personal Contributions
-	Milestone 1: Defined assumptions and summarized business and technical requirements to guide design of the IT infrastructure; aided in identifying key stakeholders and potential contingencies.
-	Milestone 2: Selected key hardware and software components for the LAN and VoIP system, ensuring scalability and reliability. Compiled technical documentation for selected components.
-	Milestone 3: Led the selection and documentation of employee hardware and software, ensuring security and compatibility with LAN and VoIP systems.
-	Milestone 4: Authored the Backup & Continuity Plan, including archival storage, off-site data retention, and redundancy planning.
-	Collaborated accross all milestones to maintain technical consistency and ensure system integration
